Privacy Policy for MixMaster

Last updated: March 2, 2026

MixMaster is a mobile application designed to provide starting-point settings for live audio mixing. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Information We Collect

MixMaster does not require user accounts, registration, or login.

We do not collect, store, or process personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or payment details.

The app may automatically receive limited technical information from your device, such as device type and operating system version, solely for app functionality and compatibility purposes.

Payments

MixMaster is purchased through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. All payment processing is handled directly by Apple or Google. We do not receive or store your payment information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent user data.

Since we do not collect personal data, we do not share personal data with third parties.

Children's Privacy

MixMaster does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Data Security

Because MixMaster does not collect or store personal information, there is no personal user data stored on external servers by us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page with a revised date.
